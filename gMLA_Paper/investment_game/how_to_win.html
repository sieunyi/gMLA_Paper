How to earn money

  • The first part of the study consists of 4 rounds of investment choices. For each choice, you can invest up to $1 in increments of $0.25.
  • Money that is not invested is yours to keep
  • You will be assigned a "success number" between 1 and 6.
  • You will roll a virtual six-sided die.
  • If the result of the die matches your "success number", the investment is successful. If your investment is successful, you get six times your investment, else you get nothing.
  • The probability of success is 1/6 (17%), while the probability of failure is 5/6 (83%).
  • In the next round, you will be assigned to a new "success number" and repeat this process.
